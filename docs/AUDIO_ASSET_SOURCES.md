# Audio Asset Sources

Codex Sleep now uses an authorization-first beta catalog. The internal beta can
test richer content locally, while every item still has an auditable source
record in `src/shared/content/audioCatalog.ts`.

## Current Beta Catalog

- 30 total local audio items.
- 10 music items.
- 8 story or guidance items.
- 12 white-noise or environment items.
- 0 `Internal beta placeholder` items.
- Local audio assets are currently about 45MB, under the 50MB beta budget.

## Source Policy

Use these sources first because their license terms are easy to inspect and
record:

- Wikimedia Commons: public domain and Creative Commons audio.
- Freesound: CC0 or CC BY only.
- LibriVox: public domain recordings only.
- Pixabay Music and Sound Effects: use only when the current Pixabay Content
  License allows the intended app distribution.
- Codex Sleep project originals: generated or recorded by this project, with
  original scripts and source notes kept here.

Avoid non-commercial licenses for a commercial app:

- Do not use CC BY-NC, CC BY-NC-SA, unclear YouTube downloads, or mirror-only
  files.
- For CC BY assets, keep attribution visible in the in-app audio credits page.
- For CC0/public domain assets, keep the source URL for auditability.

## Project-Owned Original Audio

The remaining beta items are project-owned original audio rather than
third-party placeholders:

- Music: low-frequency pads, soft piano, slow chimes, night strings, floating
  pads, and breathing-bed loops generated by the project.
- Stories and guidance: original Mandarin scripts rendered with the local
  `Microsoft Huihui Desktop` zh-CN TTS voice for beta testing.
- Noise: project-generated brown noise, pink noise, wind, creek, forest, room
  fan, rain-night, and loopable environment textures.

These are suitable for internal beta and product validation. Before public
release, do a final quality pass for loudness, loop smoothness, TTS naturalness,
and whether any project-owned generated piece should be replaced by a higher
quality commissioned or licensed recording.

## Replacement Checklist

1. Download the original source from the creator page, not a mirror.
2. Confirm the license allows app distribution and commercial use.
3. Prefer compact `.ogg` or mobile-compatible audio; keep the beta package
   budget in mind.
4. Normalize loudness so tracks do not jump in volume.
5. Update `src/shared/content/audioCatalog.ts`.
6. Keep `source.name`, `source.author`, `source.license`, `source.url`, and
   `source.attributionRequired` with every catalog item.
7. Run `npm.cmd run check` before committing.

## Future Remote Audio Direction

For a larger public catalog, move most audio to Alibaba Cloud OSS/CDN and keep
only a small local fallback pack in the app bundle. The current local catalog is
intended for internal beta richness and offline playback validation.
